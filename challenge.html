The Challenge

This challenge is about more than just miles. It is a tribute.

We are coming together to walk, run, and relay our way along the South West Coast Path in honour of our dear friend Amy, whose strength, humour, and boundless kindness left a mark on us all.

Amy was planning a sabbatical to conquer the path herself, raising funds for cancer charities following the loss of her father Keith just before she was diagnosed. When she was able, she found great solace in walking sections of the path with friends and family throughout her cancer journey.

Though she did not get the chance to complete the path herself, we will carry her spirit, and her walking poles, with us every step of the way. Together, we will finish what she started.

We are raising money for the charities that supported Amy and her loved ones throughout her illness, giving thanks for the care, compassion, and dignity they helped provide.

But this is also about bringing people together, just as Amy always did. In her memory, this challenge celebrates inclusiveness, encouragement, and joyful determination, no matter your background or ability. Whether you are covering one mile or fifty, cheering from the sidelines or passing the poles along the trail, you are part of Amy’s team.

Join us in a journey powered by resilience, friendship, and love. Let’s Be More Amy and finish the path together.
