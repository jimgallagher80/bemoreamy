BE MORE AMY
South West Coastal
Path Relay Challenge
16th to 25th May 2026

This challenge is about more than just miles—it’s a tribute.

We’re coming together to walk, run, and relay our way along the South West Coast Path in honour of our dear friend Amy, whose strength, humour, and boundless kindness left a mark on us all. Though she didn’t get the chance to complete the path herself, we’ll carry her spirit and her walking poles with us every step of the way, so that together, we finish what she started.

We’re raising money for the charities that supported Amy and her loved ones throughout her illness, giving thanks for the care, compassion, and dignity they helped provide.

But this is also about bringing people together, just as Amy always did. In her memory, this challenge celebrates inclusiveness, encouragement and joyful determination, no matter your background or ability. Whether you’re covering one mile or fifty, cheering from the sidelines or passing the poles along the trail, you are part of Amy’s team.

Join us in a journey powered by resilience, friendship, and love. Let’s ‘Be More Amy’ and finish the path together.
